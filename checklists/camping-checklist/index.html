Camping Checklist for Beginners (Printable)

Planning a camping trip? This printable checklist makes sure you bring the essential gear needed for a safe and comfortable outdoor trip.

Essential Gear

  • tent
  • sleeping bag
  • sleeping pad
  • flashlight
  • cooler
  • water container

Nice to Bring

  • camping chair
  • portable stove
  • bug spray
  • sunscreen
  • extra batteries
