Hospital Bag Checklist for Mom and Dad For Beginners

Packing your hospital bag early helps reduce stress when labor starts. This checklist includes essential items for both parents. Beginner-friendly basics.

For Mom

  • comfortable clothes
  • toiletries
  • nursing bra
  • slippers
  • phone charger

For Baby

  • baby outfit
  • blanket
  • diapers
  • wipes
